3D Models

Ancient Gauntlet

And ancient magical artifact gauntlet that was unearthed by archeologists. Modelling and retopology was done in Maya, texturing and high poly in ZBrush and Painting and Emmisive on Substance Painter. Finally the asset was imported into Unreal Engine..

Game Piece

A detailed 3D model of a game piece used in a strategy board game. A photogrammetry scan of a person (me) was used as a base model, that was then retopologized and textured in Maya and Zbrush. The final asset was then 3D printed.

RATMAN

A robotic rat character designed for fictional real-world application. The model was created using Maya for the base mesh, ZBrush for minor sculpting details, and Substance Painter for texturing. The final asset was rendered in Unreal Engine to showcase its design and materials.

Virtual Beach Terrain

A detailed 3D model of a virtual beach environment, created for use in a video game or simulation. The base terrain and masking was done in Gaea, with the final asset imported into Unreal Engine for rendering, Adding textures, dynamic water and foliage.
